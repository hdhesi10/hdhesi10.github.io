Welcome To Enver Creek Secondary's Website

Enver Creek Secondary School is a public high school located in Surrey, BC. Enver Creek is home of the cougars. Enver Creek has been open since 1997. We are located in a safe and beautiful community. We educate kids from grade 8 to grade 12

Our mission here at Enver Creek Secondary School is to provide opportunities for success and excellence as we prepare students to become confident, thoughtful, and responsible members of the community.
