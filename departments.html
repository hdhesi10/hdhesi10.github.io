Departments

Required courses at Enver Creek are English, Social Studies, Mathematics, Sciences, and Physical Education. If a student is doing extremely well in these classes we offer an honours course for most of them. English, Calculus, Art, and Chemistry offer advanced placement courses. We offer 3 language courses which are french, punjabi and spanish. Students have many options to meet the requirements of physical fitness which are weight trainging, fitness and physical education. We offer many interessting electives. They are in 2 main areas which are Applied Skills, which include Computers, Business, Woodwork, Metalwork, Electronics, Engineering, plus Foods or Cook Training. Fine Arts studies, which include Dance, Art, Media, Band, and Photography.

Math

Enver Creek offers 3 different categories for math for different careers or getting ready for post secondary. In students first year of highschool grade 8 students will have the choice of 3 different math courses. Which are advanced math which is math 8 honours, then theres regular math, and math with assistance. In grade 10 the courses divide into 2 levels of math. Students in grade 10 have a choice of apprenticeship and workplace 10 and foundations and Pre-Calculus 10 . In Math 11, the courses diverge into 3 levels where students choose Apprenticeship and Workplace 11, Foundations 11, and Pre-Calculus 11. In grade 12 math is optional for students, we offer 2 courses which are pre-calculus 12 and students who want to be prepared for post secondary can take calculus 12.
