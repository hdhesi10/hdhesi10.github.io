Clubs and Teams

The school generally offers 3 levels of individual and teams sports for both male and female students.Grade 8, junior grade 9 and 10, and senoir grade 11 and 12.Enver Creek sports teams have done extremely well in the past 10 years, due in no small part to the commitment and motivation of the students also with the dedication, passion, and knowledge of the school coaching staff.

Grad Council

Grad council is for planning and organizing grad events for our grade 12's. Senoirs in their last year of highschool have the opportunity to join grad council. Grad councils a very important club because they can plan out the events grads will have throughtout their last year. Grad council is run in room 212.

Soccer

Mr. Sujan/Mr. Green

Enver creeks soccer teams is full of very skillful soccer players. Every year our soccer team is very strong and successful. This is only because our coaches do an outstanding job getting the players prepared for the season. Soccer is avaliable to all students but they must try out and be selected for the team. Try outs are early in september in the second week of school. The soccer program has 3 teams the grade 8's, the juniors which is grade 9/10's, and the senior team 11/12's. All of our teams are outstanding. Our senior team won provincials in the year 2011/2012.

Weight Training

Mr. Sujan/Mr. Green

Students who would like to workout and stay fit can join the weight training club. This club is to exercise and train. This club runs in the weight room on M/Tu/Th/F 7:30 to 8:15am Tu/W/Th 3:15 to 4:15 pm. This club is perfect for students who cannot go to a gym and want to workout. Joining weight trainging can help make you fit or even enhance it even more. This club is a perfect way to stay fit and healthy.
